Vegan Chili

A Mean Bean Satiating Machine

This chili is a great hearty meal if you feel like you want to cut back on meat and have an hour to prep a few ingredients. If you meal prep the way I do, you can eat this throughout the week and it will taste pretty great.

Ingredients

  • 1 Red Onion, chopped fine
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Red Pepper, cubed
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 2 Tins Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 cup Chickpeas, cooked
  • 1 cup Red Kidney Beans, cooked
  • 1 cup Sweetcorn, cooked
  • 1 cup Black Beans, cooked

How it's done

  1. Oil up a pot, olive oil works and get it to medium or medium-low heat. (if you're slow at dicing you can wait to do this after step 2)
  2. Alright now you gotta dice the onion. Be warned you will cry a lot (I learned this the hard way)
  3. If you've made it this far its now time to mince, crush, or smash some garlic cloves.
  4. Now throw your diced onions and crushed garlic in the pot and get it sweaty
  5. While that's cooking dice the red pepper. You can do it in chunks or strips, really up to you.
  6. Add cumin, cinammon, and cayenne to a bowl
  7. Throw your red pepper and spices into the pot and mix it around, it should smell pretty nice
  8. Keep mixing for a few minutes, while thats prepping add chickpeas, corn, kidney beans, and black beans to a bowl
  9. Now grab your chopped tomatoes and add them to the pot and throw your bean mix in as well
  10. Let that cook for around 30 minutes keep stiring so you don't burn the bottom
  11. That's it! Let it cool and eat it up
