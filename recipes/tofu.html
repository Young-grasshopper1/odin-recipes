Tofu Forever

I eat tofu almost daily. After pressing to get all the water out, I just pan sear and douse in soy sauce and some other flavors

Ingredients

  • 1 block of tofu
  • 3/4 cup of soy sauche
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 tablespoon of ginger
  • 1 green onion link
  • teaspoon of oil

How it's made

  1. Press the liquid out of the block of tofu.
  2. Cut the tofu into cubes
  3. Warm a pan on medium hear and add oil
  4. Cook tofu on pan and get it to brown
  5. Lower heat and add soy sauce to tofu
  6. Reduce the soy sauce until tofu gets a thick coating
  7. toss in ginger and garlic
  8. Cut up green onion and toss into pan
  9. Serve tofu
